Project Description

A home-wall is a miniature climbing wall that's installed in someone's home, garage, backyard, or a shed. They're generally used for training when you don't have time to go to the climbing gym or to go climb outdoors. This is a project that I'm currently in the midst of working on - although I haven't yet started construction I have obtained most of the holds I'll need for the wall and a large portion of the lumber. I also have made design schematics, however, before I start building the wall I want to get these schematics proof-checked by some form of structural engineer. The picture to the left is just a generic picture of a backyard homewall that has a similar construction style to the wall I aspire to build.
